Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Mapshare, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we handle data on our website.

Data Collection and Usage

We want to assure you that Mapshare does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. Our service is designed to function without the need for user accounts or the retention of any user-specific information.

  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.
  • We do not collect or store your IP address.
  • We do not track your browsing behavior on our site or elsewhere on the internet.
  • Any location data entered is used only for the immediate function of providing a mapcode and is not stored or retained after your session ends.

GDPR Compliance

Mapshare is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union. As we do not collect or process personal data, many GDPR requirements do not apply to our service. However, we are committed to the principles of data protection and privacy that the GDPR represents.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Maps for displaying map information. Please note that Google may collect data about your interaction with the map feature. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy for more information about their data practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at mapshare@mruta.uk.
